Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our rooms and booking process

What time is check-in and check-out?

+

Check-in time is from 14:00 (2:00 PM), and check-out is until 12:00 (noon). Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability.

Is breakfast included with the room?

+

Most of our room packages include complimentary breakfast. You can check if breakfast is included in the room description. Breakfast is served from 6:30 AM to 10:30 AM daily.

Can I request a specific room view?

+

Yes, you can request a specific room view during booking. We offer ocean view, garden view, pool view, and mountain view rooms. While we do our best to accommodate preferences, specific views are subject to availability.

What is the cancellation policy?

+

For most room types, free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before check-in. Some special rates and promotions may have different cancellation policies. The specific policy will be displayed before you complete your booking.

Is there free WiFi in the rooms?

+

Yes, all rooms include complimentary high-speed WiFi internet access. WiFi is also available in all public areas of the resort.

Do you offer airport transfers?

+

Yes, we offer airport transfer services for an additional fee. You can arrange this when booking your room or by contacting our concierge at least 24 hours before your arrival.
