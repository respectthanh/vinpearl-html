About Vinpearl Nha Trang

Discover our story, our vision, and the team behind Vietnam's premier luxury resort experience.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming one of Vietnam's most prestigious resort destinations.

Nestled on the pristine Hon Tre Island in Nha Trang Bay, Vinpearl Resort & Spa Nha Trang was established in 2003 as the first luxury resort in Vietnam. Since then, we have grown to become a symbol of luxury hospitality in Vietnam, welcoming thousands of guests from around the world.

With a commitment to exceptional service, breathtaking natural beauty, and world-class amenities, Vinpearl Nha Trang offers an unparalleled resort experience. Our resort spans across 200 hectares of lush tropical gardens, white sand beaches, and crystal-clear waters.

As part of the Vingroup - one of Vietnam's largest conglomerates - Vinpearl continues to set the standard for luxury hospitality in Vietnam, combining Vietnamese warmth and hospitality with international standards of service.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Vinpearl Nha Trang, we are dedicated to preserving the natural beauty of our environment and supporting local communities.

We have implemented numerous sustainable practices across our resort operations, including energy-efficient systems, water conservation measures, and waste reduction programs. Our goal is to minimize our environmental footprint while maximizing the positive impact we have on the local community.

Some of our key sustainability initiatives include:

  • Reducing single-use plastics through our comprehensive plastic-free program
  • Supporting local farmers and producers through our farm-to-table dining program
  • Implementing energy-saving technologies across all resort facilities
  • Protecting and preserving local marine ecosystems through coral restoration projects
  • Engaging in community development projects and educational programs
