Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our tours and experiences.

What should I bring on tours?

For most tours, we recommend bringing sunscreen, hat, sunglasses, comfortable walking shoes, swimwear (for water activities), a light jacket or cover-up, camera, and cash for personal expenses. Specific tour requirements will be provided upon booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the scheduled tour receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24-48 hours receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 24 hours in advance or no-shows are not refundable.

Are meals included in the tours?

Most full-day tours include lunch, and some special tours include additional meals as specified in the tour description. Beverages are typically not included unless explicitly mentioned.

Are the tours suitable for children?

Many of our tours are family-friendly and suitable for children. Each tour description indicates the recommended age groups and activity level. For specific concerns, please contact our tour concierge for guidance.

How do I book a tour?

You can book tours online through our website, by phone, or through the resort concierge. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to secure your spot, especially during high season.
